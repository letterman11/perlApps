CONVERTIBLE BOND PARAMETERS
---------------------------

A) CONVERSION RATIO: # OF SHARES ONE PAR VALUE BOND CONVERTS INTO. $1000 PAR IBM => 67 COMMON SHARES

B) CONVERSION VALUE: COMMON STOCK PRICE * CONVERSION RATIO.  $80 * 67 = $5360

C) STRAIGHT VALUE: DISCOUNTED CASH FLOWS OF CONVERTIBLE BOND IGNORING EMBEDDED CONVERSION OPTIONS

D) MARKET CONVERSION PRICE: IMPLICIT PRICE PER SHARE OF UNDERLYING COMMON SHARE GIVEN PURCHASE PRICE OF CONVERTIBLE BOND

BUSTED CONVERTIBLE
------------------
JUNK STOCK ASSOCIATED WITH CONVERTIBLE BOND CONVERSION VALUE IS LESS THAN 50% OF PRICE OF CONVERTIBLE BOND

E) PARITY PRICE: COMMON STOCK PRICE * CONVERSION RATIO / 10

F) PREMIUM POINTS: BOND PRICE - PARITY PRICE

G) PERCENT PREMIUM: (BOND PRICE - PARITY PRICE) / PARITY PRICE * 100

H) ADVANTAGE: DIFFERENCE IN CURRENT YIELD IN CONVERT BOND AND DIVIDEND YIELD IN STOCK
