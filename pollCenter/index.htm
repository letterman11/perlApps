• HOME

    Poll Center: An Ajax/Web 2.0 application that implements a basic polling program that graphically displays the results of each poll.


  • ABOUT

    The technology platform behind Poll Center is based on HTML/CSS/Javascript and Jquery/JqPlot. The site is driven on the backend by Perl/Mysql. Location data is also culled from the submittion by each pollee.


  • SOLUTIONS

    The principal developer of this site is available for contract or full-time work, having over 8 years of experience in a variety of technologies spanning from Javascript to Perl to C++ and Mysql.
